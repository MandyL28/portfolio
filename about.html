Hi, I'm Mandy. Once upon a time, I had a head full of historical research. How would I get it out to the wider world?

Make a website, of course.

So I picked up Lynda Weinman's big yellow book of HTML and taught myself how to make a website. The site is still going strong today. Check it out here.

I've been SO fortunate. It caught attention and led me to the opportunity to talk about the royal family on British and North American media. So yes, the marriage between code and content creation has been magical. :)
